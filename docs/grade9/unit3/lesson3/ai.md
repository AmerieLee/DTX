---

# Lab Overview

Artificial Intelligence (AI) can explain complex ideas, answer questions, and provide examples.

In this lab, you will use AI to help you understand **network protocols**. You will also learn how to write clear prompts, check the accuracy of AI responses, and improve AI-generated explanations.

Remember:

**AI is a learning assistant, not a replacement for your own thinking.**

---

# Learning Objectives

By the end of this lab, you will be able to:

- write effective prompts
- ask AI for clear explanations
- compare AI responses with your textbook
- identify inaccurate or incomplete information
- improve AI-generated answers
- explain network protocols in your own words

---

# Responsible Use of AI

When using AI:

✅ Ask clear questions.

✅ Check information using reliable sources.

✅ Think critically about every answer.

✅ Rewrite ideas in your own words.

Never:

❌ Copy AI responses without understanding them.

❌ Assume every AI response is completely correct.

❌ Submit AI-generated work as your own without following your teacher's instructions.

- the audience
- the topic
- the level of detail
- the desired format
- any examples you want

Use simple English and one real-life example.
