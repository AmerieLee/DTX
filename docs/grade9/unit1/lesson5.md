# Lesson 5: Responsible Digital Citizens

## Big Question
How can we use technology safely, ethically and responsibly?

## Learning Goals
- Explain digital citizenship.
- Protect personal information.
- Recognise cyberbullying and misinformation.
- Use AI responsibly.

## Starter
Discuss: Should everything online be trusted?

## Topics
### Digital Footprint
Everything you do online can leave a record.

### Privacy
Never share passwords or personal information.

### Cyberbullying
Report, block and support others.

### AI Ethics
Check AI answers before using them.

## Activity
Evaluate three online posts and decide if they are trustworthy.

## AI Lab
Ask an AI to summarise an article. Verify the summary using the original source.

## Mini Project
Create a one-page Digital Citizenship Guide for new Grade 9 students.

## Reflection
What habit will you change after today's lesson?
